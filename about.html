Always curious about art, illustration and animation, I discovered that I could spend hours behind a computer colouring drawings or photoshopping pictures.
Graduated as a Bachelor in Graphic Design in Buenos Aires and obtained a Master Degree in Kyoto, Japan.
Worked for some local companies in Argentina and Japan, though currently I spend most of my time doing freelance work and participating in - and winning- design contests.
Inspired by everything unusual, mysterious or bizarre. I like to work both alone and in groups.
